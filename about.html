Min Park

Designer

hi there,

My name is Min Park, and I'm a student studying architecture design at Yonsei University.

I have a great affection for people, so I tend to really use that emotion as a motivation when it comes to architectural design. I truly and strongly feel the desire that I’d like to somehow make a difference in people’s lives. And this very desire is what keeps me going.

While valuing user-centered architecture, I feel the utmost importance of making architectural proposals that fit the urban context which is why I am also striving to study urban planning as well. My deepest interest lays in public space – for anyone, meaning without any kind of discrimination – or especially for certain groups of people who are rather unseen and forgotten in our society. Therefore, in addition to intentionally planned urban spaces, looking out for significant social spaces naturally generated by users and members themselves is another intriguing point I am focusing on. I hope to become a designer who constantly think about the social role and the sustainability of architecture.
