What does 'aboutBooks' do?

'aboutBooks' aims to help children read books with artificial intelligence for all parents in Korea who work and raise a child at the same time.
What we noticed as a problem was that although at home, children are provided with much support and encouragement to form healthy reading habits, very few of them are actually interested in reading books. On the other hand, when adults choose their favorite content from services such as YouTube and Netflix, each service recommends customized content again, and as this process repeats, users are locked-in the service. However, there is no such service for children's reading.
Therefore, to explain what 'aboutBooks' do, customized books for each child are curated using artificial intelligence recommendation models and mutual user-based content consumption algorithms.


What kind of problems are we solving?

路 The average purchase of children's books (20 books) costs more than 300,000 won, but the average reads all of them in one month, and about 10% of them are frequently read by children.
路 By applying rental and selective purchasing models, it reduces waste from purchasing and storing unnecessary books and helps reasonable consumption.
路 Although working parents are interested in their children's education, it is difficult to know which book to choose because they do not have enough time and do not know their children's tastes.
路 Due to the COVID-19 situation, families are reluctant to visit multi-use facilities such as libraries and bookstores, so non-face-to-face delivery methods are used.
