Housing Project

House Bites




2019

Architecture Studio 1

When we refer to ‘Home Sweet Home’, we tend to imagine only the cozy inside part of a house. Why can’t outside space be also part of the cozy house we were thinking of? Here is a solution to repeat inside and outside elements(in out in out in), smoothly divided by the curvy roof (U).

Then, why is bringing outside in so important? Outside space inevitably turns into gathering space. There are no walls, hardly any privacy. You can be seen, but you can also see others at the same time. In the modern world, it is much easier to scatter than to come together. Numerous outside spaces repeating in the house can naturally increase chances of the family to stumble on each other and consequently bring them together more often.




Every room was not NAMED for its purpose,

but the space itself CLAIMED its own usage.
