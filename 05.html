Stack Health Examination Center

for everyone;

2019

Architecture Studio 2








Material Detail

Examination rooms were designed to be solid- unseen and the hallways to be transparent. This not only allowed the building to gain lightness, but also these transparent hallways are transition spaces which can give the people a more pleasant experience of waiting times. However, all hallways cannot be seen through in regard of privacy. Therefore, transparency varies in 3 steps: see through, silhouette, and solid. Therefore, especially on 1st floor the space created is very internally oriented and there seems to be a boundary of solid silhouette and see through. Since the very outer boundary is solid, the height of the hallways was built extra higher so that sunlight could come in easier.
